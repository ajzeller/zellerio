Background

I took my passion for personal finance to learn Sketch App and redesign the Mint personal personal finance app made by Intuit.

Design

Compared to the existing Mint app, this design focuses on putting more relevant personal finance statistics at the user's fingertips. Some highlights include an overview page with useful at-a-glance monthly and overall finance stats and a trends page with a graph inspired by Coinbase.
